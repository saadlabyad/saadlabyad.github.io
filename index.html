I hold a PhD in Mathematics from the University of Oxford, where I was supervised by Professor Samuel N. Cohen and Professor Álvaro Cartea, under the Ioan and Rosemary James scholarship, with support from the Alan Turing Institute.

My research focuses on statistical learning of point processes — in particular Hawkes processes and their non-stationary extensions — with applications to high-frequency financial data, including mid-price modelling and market impact in equity limit order books.

I am currently seeking quantitative research and quantitative trading roles at hedge funds, proprietary trading firms, and asset managers.
